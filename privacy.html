Privacy Policy — Personal Clip Publisher

Last updated: July 2026

Personal Clip Publisher ("the app") is a single-user tool used by one individual to publish their own short-form videos to their own TikTok account.

What data the app handles

The app handles only the account owner's own TikTok access token, obtained through TikTok's official OAuth flow. This token is stored locally on the owner's own computer and is used solely to upload the owner's own videos to the owner's own TikTok account via TikTok's official Content Posting API.

What the app does NOT do

The app does not collect, store, share, or sell personal data from any other person. It has no other users. It does not access, aggregate, or process any third party's content or data. It performs no tracking or analytics on any individual.

Data sharing

No data is shared with any third party, except the requests the app makes directly to TikTok's official API to upload the owner's own videos.
